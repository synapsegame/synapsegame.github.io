A mobile rougelike shoot em' up for fans of science fiction, arena shooters, looting, crafting, and a good challenge. Currently in development for iOS and Android.

Survive

Your hero is not the best pilot in the universe, but is quite skilled at surviving crash landings. Planets are generally hostile, but stocked with useful resources. Crash, mine, survive, and escape your way throughout the multiverse.

Explore

Some progression carries between planets, allowing you to customize your hero to fit your own playstyle. Extract resources and upgrade your abilities to survive greater challenges.

Iterate

Each planet may yield unique discoveries in each playthrough. Explore planets for broad discovery. Conquer planets for unique rewards.
