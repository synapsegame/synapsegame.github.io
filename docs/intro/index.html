About Synapse

My primary goal with Synapse is to make a game that I enjoy playing. Arena shooters and rougelites work well with my attention span while still offering lots of replayability. I also really appreciate constant progression, which probably comes from growing up on RPGs. Loot hits the reward center just right. And crafting can allow you to tailor things to your own play style.

I'm not a fan of games that push ads relentlessly. It really hurts any flow that might build up through the session. So, that will never happen.

I also detest games that force some energy bar on you that depletes as you play the game, eventually causing you to hit a wall where you stop and let it recharge, or spend money to buy more credits.

Regarding monetization: It would be nice to start turning this into something I can do full time. I have some ideas on how cosmetics and other functionality could fit in to the game without making it a pay-to-win bore. After all, the goal is to make something that's fun to play, and fun to keep coming back to. It'll be primarily single player (not sure about anything multiplayer at this time), so at most, players might be able to purchase resources to upgrade items in runs. Still not sure what the approach will be here.

I do also plan to release this on Steam (perfect for a SteamDeck), likely in 2024. Still not sure how I feel about doing early access, since the whole idea behind early access has been replaced by buggy cash grabs.
